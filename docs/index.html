About and Features

About Music Server Stuff Planned features

This bot is designed to provide the best user experience possible tottaly free, as time goes by adding new and exciting features; built from scratch, the bot will evolve over time so join the community and communicate that you would like to see in the bot, all ideas are welcome.

Some of the current features of the bot are the possibility to see the avatars of the members of your server for download, to listen to music from any title or playlist of youtube (we are still working on the playback from livestreams). all detailed in this list.

Give to your voice chat a bit of rhythm with music, or share with your friends the podcast that you like directly from youtube in discord, with this command you can play or request any video or playlist of youtube including those that like the recommended playlists of spoglight or that which is your own.

Stalker would like to know your musical taste so you can ask him to look for a song through the video link, or simply by name. We know that you have many options for this type of functions so if this does not convince you observe the rest.

Now that you saw how to order music, you will need the legendary art of consulting what music is currently in the queue for playback if you want to know when it will be the turn of your favorite song or who requested the legendary soundtrack of your favorite game.

Stalker also worries that your chat will always look as orderly as possible, so if there are thousands of songs or just five, stalker will show them 10 in 10 in different pages, all done in such a way that you do not have to go down to the infinite and beyond for the song you were looking for. On the other hand of the same toast we know that it is boring to remember in which page you stayed so we have a beautiful interactive menu in the lower part so that you can change the tab by just clicking.
